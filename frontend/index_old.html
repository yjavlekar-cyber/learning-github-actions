ABOUT:

Hailing from Mumbai, Profound is part of a new wave of independent hip-hop artists shaping the city’s evolving underground sound. With a discography rooted in singles and consistent releases, his music reflects a raw, unfiltered perspective blending introspection, street narratives, and contemporary rap influences.

Rather than chasing trends, Profound’s approach is grounded in authenticity. His tracks carry a stripped-down, honest energy—where storytelling meets mood driven production, creating a sound that feels both personal and relatable. Each release adds another layer to his identity, building a catalogue that speaks to growth, struggle, and self-expression.

Emerging from one of India’s most culturally diverse cities, Profound draws inspiration from Mumbai’s fast-paced life and contrasting realities. His music captures that tension between ambition and survival, individuality and environment making his sound resonate with listeners navigating similar journeys.
